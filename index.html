Should I get a Covid Test?

Our goal is to provide you with a simple tool to find out if you shoud get a Covid test.
We hope to make testing more accessible by helping you identify testing sites close to you.
Please begin by answering the questions below.

Are you experiencing any of these symptoms?

  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Fever or chills
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headache
  • New loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion or runny nose
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea

You should get a Covid test as soon as possible. Please refer to the map below.
Proceed to next question.

Have you been in close contact for more than 15 minutes with someone who has COVID-19?

You should get a Covid test as soon as possible. Please refer to the map below.
